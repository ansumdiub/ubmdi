Materials Data Engineering Laboratory (MaDE@UB)

Transforming Databases from Library to Serve as a Value-added Materials Data Laboratory

Made@UB is a web portal for knowledge discovery in databases (KDD) associated with a broad range of materials science, which enhances the functionalities of databases from one of search and retrieval to instead achieve knowledge discovery across a variety of materials platforms. This cyberinfrastructure is organized in a series of data building blocks, which includes the tool kit for a large variety of informatic methods, computational and experimental literature based datasets, and a reference /bibliography to host the provinence associated with a variety of problems.
