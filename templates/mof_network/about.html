Materials Data Engineering Laboratory

A web portal for knowledge discovery in database associated with a broad range technological and social issues that are influenced by materials science

Transforming Databases from Library to Serve as a Value-added Materials Data Laboratory

MaDE@UB is a synergistic data platform for knowledge discovery in databases (KDD), applicable to a diverse genre of data associated with technological and social issues in material science.

This cyber-infrastructure provides access to a series of data infrastructure building blocks (DIBBs) which include a toolkit built on materials informatics methods, a bibliography of provenance data, and which welcomes contributions of data and tools to apply KDD methods to increase the dimensionality of information. MaDE@UB enhances the functionalities of databases from one of search and retrieval to instead achieve Knowledge Discovery across a variety of material platforms (ie. from data library to data laboratory), and aims to converge disciplines ranging from physical sciences, life sciences and social sciences.
